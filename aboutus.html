Frequently Asked Questions


What types of events do you organize?
We organize various events including corporate events, weddings, social gatherings, and more.

How can I contact you to discuss my event?
You can contact us through our website's contact page or by phone. We'll be happy to discuss your event requirements.

I'm concerned about the logistics of organizing a team retreat
We understand that organizing a team retreat can be overwhelming, which is why we offer a comprehensive planning service to help you every step of the way. From choosing the perfect location to designing a custom program and handling all the details, our team of experts will work with you to create a seamless and stress-free experience. We’ll handle all the logistics so you can focus on what’s most important: your team.

I'm not sure if a team retreat is right for my team
We believe that team retreats can be beneficial for any team, no matter the size or industry. Our retreats are designed to help teams connect, collaborate, and build stronger relationships, which can lead to improved communication, increased motivation, and more innovative ideas. We offer a variety of options to fit different needs and budgets, so we’re confident that we can help you find the perfect retreat for your team.

When should I pay my Team Retreat?
In order to ensure availability and pricing accuracy, we recommend to our clients to pay at least 30 days before the starting date of the Team Retreat. Our payment terms are as below: 50% = down payment at the signature of the contract (booking of the venues) 40% = 21 days before the starting date of the retreat (booking of the activities) 10% + any additional charges encountered (final balance) = within 2 weeks after the end of the retreat.
